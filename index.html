About Sustainable Development Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all." They were set up in 2015 by the United Nations General Assembly and are intended to be achieved by 2030.

Our Interactive Demos:

  • SDG 2 - Zero Hunger: Recipe Recommender helps reduce food waste and promote sustainable eating
  • SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-being: Mood Journal supports mental health tracking and awareness
  • SDG 4 - Quality Education: AI Study Buddy enhances learning through personalized flashcards

Each demo showcases how AI technology can contribute to achieving these important global goals through practical, accessible tools.
